Why FloodWatch Kerala?

🌊
Live Water Level Monitoring
Real-time water level data from all 14 districts, auto-updated every 15 seconds. No manual sync needed.
🚨
Instant Flood Alerts
Severity-based alerts (Low to Extreme) issued directly by district authorities for immediate public action.
📋
Community Flood Reporting
Citizens can report flood situations with photos. Admins verify and dispatch rescue teams within minutes.
🚁
Rescue Team Coordination
Track NDRF, SDRF, Fire & Rescue, Coast Guard teams across all 14 districts with real-time availability.
📡
14 District Control Rooms
Direct contact numbers for every district collector office, EOC and fire station across Kerala.
🌧️
Rainfall & Weather Data
Live rainfall readings, temperature and humidity for every monitoring station, updated automatically.
